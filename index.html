About Us

The MTSU DEI Support Project was created to provide clear, accessible resources for students, faculty, and staff navigating changes to diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives on campus.

We believe that every member of our community deserves support, representation, and a voice. Our mission is to bridge gaps, facilitate understanding, and promote an environment where all perspectives are valued and heard.

Through curated resources, open dialogue, and community partnerships, we work to ensure that MTSU remains a welcoming place for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

Understanding DEI

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) ensures that every individual, regardless of background, feels valued, respected, and supported in their educational and professional environment.

Our goal is to provide clarity, educational tools, and resources that help promote fairness and representation for everyone at MTSU.
