Vision, Mission, and Values

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion

Vision

We envision a campus community where every individual—regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, ability, or background—feels a genuine sense of belonging. Our vision is a Middle Tennessee State University where diversity is celebrated, equity is embedded in every policy and practice, and inclusion is the foundation of our academic and social culture.

We strive to be a model institution that demonstrates how higher education can lead the way in creating meaningful change, fostering dialogue, and building bridges across differences.

Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, actionable resources and support to students, faculty, and staff navigating the evolving landscape of diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives at MTSU. We serve as a bridge between institutional policy changes and the lived experiences of our community members.

Through education, advocacy, and collaboration, we work to ensure that every voice is heard, every perspective is valued, and every member of our community has the tools they need to thrive in an inclusive environment.

Values

  • Respect: We honor the dignity and worth of every individual.
  • Equity: We advocate for fair treatment, access, and opportunities for all.
  • Transparency: We believe in open communication and honest dialogue.
  • Empowerment: We provide resources that enable individuals to advocate for themselves and others.
  • Collaboration: We partner with campus and community organizations to amplify our impact.
  • Accountability: We hold ourselves and our institution to the highest standards of integrity.
