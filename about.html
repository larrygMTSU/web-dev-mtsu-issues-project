Welcome to MTSU DEI Support

When & Why We Started

The MTSU DEI Support Project was launched in Fall 2024 as a collaborative group initiative to address the evolving landscape of diversity, equity, and inclusion resources on campus. Our team recognized a critical need for accessible, centralized information following recent policy changes affecting DEI programs. We created this platform to ensure that every student, faculty member, and staff has clear guidance and support during this transitional period.

Three Main Reasons

  1. Accessibility: To provide a single, easy-to-navigate hub for DEI resources that serves the entire MTSU community.
  2. Transparency: To clearly communicate changes, resources, and support options without bias or ambiguity.
  3. Community Empowerment: To equip students and staff with tools and knowledge to advocate for inclusive practices and understand their rights.
