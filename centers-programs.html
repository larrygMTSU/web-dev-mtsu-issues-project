Centers & Programs

Campus and external organizations advancing inclusion, support, and equity.

Campus-Based

  • Example Center for Inclusive Excellence (This office promotes inclusive excellence, handling diversity, equity, and compliance at MTSU.)
  • Student Support & Advocacy Office (The Student Success Center helps students with study skills, time management, supplemental instruction (tutoring), and overall academic success.)
  • Accessibility & Learning Services (MTSU's Disability & Access Center provides academic accommodations, advocacy, and programs to support students with disabilities.)

External Partners (Nashville/Murfreesboro Area)

  • Urban League of Middle Tennessee (A partnership with 9Paths offering a 12-month program that bridges the gap in advancement and visibility for African Americans in Corporate America and the small business community through diversity, inclusion, and equitable practices.)
  • The Table Action (A social action initiative promoting community engagement and social justice.)
  • Center for Nonprofit Excellence of Middle Tennessee (An organization committed to creating an equitable, inclusive, and collaborative space for nonprofits by embracing diversity across all backgrounds and courageously addressing biases to increase access to their programs and initiatives.)
